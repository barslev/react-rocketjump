What is ReactRocketJump?

The power and beauty of redux wrapped into a reusable container with a declarative syntax.

Flexible

Rocketjump has no implicit dependencies except React>=16.8. State and side effects are bound locally to your Component, so you can use rocketjump without having to rewrite your entire app.

You can use it side-by-side to something like redux and maybe reuse stuff like selectors, reducers and api calls.

Reusable

Rocketjump uses composition in order to let you build a reusable api. Rocketjump objects are reusable blueprint so you can easy share your behaviors with no effort
